Send a prefilled email to your chosen spokesperson

When you click the button below, your email provider of choice will open with the subject and body prefilled. The capitalized words are what you customize accordingly, and also don't forget to change the email to the representative's.

Other Ways To Connect:

Call Members of Congress Via Jewish Voice for Peace

JVP have a direct link to connect you to your congress representative after filling in some information. When you click "Call me" via their link, you will receive a phone call on the number you provided and be patched through to your representatives. You will be able to view a provided script at that time. After you deliver a message, press * (star) to be connected to your next representative, until you've called all three representatives. If you don't get through, it is recommended to leave a voicemail.

Email Your Congressmen via American Friends Service Committee

With the link below, AFSC has provided a prefilled email explaining why the congress members you are emailing should call immediately for a ceasefire. All you have to do is fill out some basic information so they are able to put you into contact with the correct people.
