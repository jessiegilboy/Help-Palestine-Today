How many innocent children must DIE before you act?

Things you can do today help end the ongoing genocide of Palestinian people:

Educate Yourself and Others

In a world where information is (literally) within an arm's reach away, it is your responsibility to educate yourself using reliable, informative sources. Gone are the days where ignorance as an excuse is acceptable when everyday, innocent people are being maimed, murdered, and displaced.

Donate Directly to Gaza

Palestinians are currently trapped within Israeli-made borders throughout Palestine, without food or safe routes to exit. Making sure your money will go directly into Gaza, whether through a trustworthy organization (as listed below) or to a local family is important.

Contact Your Local Representatives to Demand a Ceasefire

Below is a link for you to locate and contact your local representatives. You can email or call them to demand that they immediately call for ceasefire. It is important to have as many officials in office supporting a ceasefire, and the more citizens that contact them, the more helpful it is to the cause.

Sign Your Name to These Petitions to Bring Justice to the People of Palestine

The revolution of digital activism has made it easier to have the population's voices heard to decision-makers in office. Not only do they help spread awareness of the cause, but it is also an easy way to show your support for Palestine.
